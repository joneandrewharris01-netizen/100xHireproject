Question: would you take a qualified intro/meeting at $[X] per accepted intro,
paid on acceptance regardless of close? I'd front the qualification (call notes,
ICP fit, budget signal). You take the meeting and run your own process from there.

If yes, I have [N] candidates ready this month. Happy to start with one as a trial.

Jone Andrew Harris
linkedin.com/in/jone-andrew-harris

## Operational rules

1. **Verify BD identity before DMing** — confirm contact still at firm.
2. **Track replies in `agents/pe_intel/queue/`** — each DM = one queue entry.
3. **Trial first.** Never agree to "10 meetings/month" upfront. ONE intro, get paid, then scale.
4. **Get paid on intro acceptance, not close.** "$X per accepted qualified intro" not "% of closed deal."
5. **1-page written agreement.** Rate, qualified-intro definition, payment terms (Wise/Stripe NET-7), no exclusivity.
6. **Skip "edge — large" firms** unless warm intro: STX Next, AOE, Avidly, Cresta, Quantium, CIENCE, Abstrakt, CohnReznick, Citrin Cooperman, Aprio.
7. **Capital raise / fundraise referrals — DO NOT.** US SEC requires BD registration.
8. **Cross-reference with `pe_intel.db`.** Tag oversized leads with a `broker_to` candidate firm.
